Charo is a digital platform developed to bridge elders and families in Samtse with RSSC headquarters in Thimphu, helping reduce isolation and ensuring dignity through ICT-based support.

Mission & Vision

  • To provide simple, accessible, and inclusive digital support for elders and their families.
  • A compassionate Bhutan where elders live with dignity, care, and connection.

Why Samtse?

Samtse has one of the largest elderly populations in Bhutan. Many elders face loneliness and limited access to services when their children are away. This platform serves as a bridge between RSSC headquarters in Thimphu and elders in Samtse, ensuring timely care, awareness, and community connection.
